# REPRESENTATION-COMPARISON-V1

Purpose: compare ATOMS-V1 and EVA under matched out-of-sample local-context prediction.

This is not a decipherment claim and not a proof of optimality. It asks whether one representation gives more predictable held-out symbol sequences under the same protocol.

## Split

- Train folios: `f1r`, `f1v`, `f47v`.
- Test folios: `f2r`, `f2v`.
- Smoothing: fixed Lidstone `alpha=0.5`, fitted from train only.
- Predicted opportunities: internal symbols only, excluding first and last symbols of each sequence.
- Comparison rows: paired molecules only, requiring both an ATOMS signature and an aligned exported EVA token with at least three symbols.

This V1 comparison is therefore a matched aligned-subset test, not yet a complete-EVA-folio test.

## Tokenization

- ATOMS: ATOMS signatures are whitespace-separated ATOMS-V1 labels.
- EVA: EVA tokens are scored as raw character sequences from the exported EVA token string.

## Local-Context Model

For each internal symbol, the model uses only:

- immediate left symbol;
- immediate right symbol;
- positional role;
- sequence length.

All context distributions, vocabularies, and smoothing denominators are estimated from train only.

## Matched Corruption Test

For each held-out sequence, 100 corrupted alternatives were generated by shuffling only internal symbols while preserving sequence length, first/last symbols, and the internal symbol multiset.

| Representation | Scope | Sequences | Fraction real lower than corrupted | Mean real-minus-corrupted bits |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| ATOMS | `combined` | 14 | 47.00% | Infinity |
| ATOMS | `f2r` | 6 | 50.00% | Infinity |
| ATOMS | `f2v` | 8 | 44.75% | Infinity |
| EVA | `combined` | 14 | 33.43% | -0.965052 |
| EVA | `f2r` | 6 | 35.00% | -1.064412 |
| EVA | `f2v` | 8 | 32.25% | -0.890531 |

Negative real-minus-corrupted margin means the real sequence receives lower code length than corrupted alternatives.

## Interpretation

EVA has lower combined normalized held-out log-loss than ATOMS under this matched local-context protocol.

EVA has higher combined top-1 accuracy.

EVA has a lower unseen-context rate.

Zero-probability held-out events: ATOMS=2, EVA=0.
